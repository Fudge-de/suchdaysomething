Personal blog

I come from Bhuj, Kutch, and am very close to my roots. The place has given me my love for nature, quiet spaces, and has allowed me to self reflect, away from the noises of cities. I've been peeking into the voices in me, and somewhere in there I've found my love for design, to express myself fully, to be okay with being quirky, colorful, adding a little bit of funk to how I go about life.

As an aspiring designer, I'm always looking forward to new things to keep me engaged. When you truly love and are interested in doing something, you tap into a flow state where the time stops ticking, external voices blur out, and all you can do is focus on the process. You don't realise what you've made untill it's done and you look at the final product and say...well! The quest is like a vessel that's never going to be full, I'll always want to seek more, to know more. I am quite expressive as a person and whatever it is that I feel, reflects very easily in my actions.
I am the kind of a person who would be ever ready with packed bags for a trek to the mountains. I wish to reflect my love and respect for mother nature and the connection that I feel with her in my work, and aspire to be capable enough to impart these feelings via the art platform.
